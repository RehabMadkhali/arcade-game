Classic Arcade Game!

How To Play?

It's an old classic game, the only thing you need to do is :
1- press righ to turn right.
2- press left to turn left.
3- press up to go up.
4- press down to go down.

Note:

Becareful! try avoiding colliding with enemies, they are danguoris.. they will take your life!. so make sure to go to the other side (to the water) while you're safe!
