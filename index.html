Classic Arcade Game!

How To Play?

This's a version of the classic arcade game .. its main idea is to reach to the water safe! but this's is MY VERSION so here's my rules!
Your :
- As you can see, your already charged with 10 points . as much as you achieve your goal reaching the water .. your heart will expand.
- As much as you collect ★ , your heart will expand.
- As much as you collide with , your heart will shrink.
- whenever you lost all your points, you will lose one of your life chances.
